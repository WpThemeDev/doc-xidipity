#title#

#iframe#

The iframe code is obtained by clicking the (•••) link to the lower right of the video. Select embed and optionally advanced settings to customize the presentation. Facebook videos typically do not conform to a 16x9 aspect ratio. The template defaults to a 1x1 ratio for mobile and 16x10 for standard media. Supported settings includ the following:

  • @1x1
  • @3x2
  • @4x3
  • @7x5
  • @16x9
  • @16x10
  • @21x9
