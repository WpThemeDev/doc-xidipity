definition

The terms “color” and “hue” are often used interchangeably by artists and designers. While, for all intents and purposes, this will get you by, the words “color” and “hue” actually mean different things. In general, “color” is used to refer to all, well, colors, including black, white and grey. While “hue” refers to the origin of the color we see. It is the base of the color we see and is always one of the six primary and secondary colors on the color wheel.
