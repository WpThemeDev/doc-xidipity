Description:Classes for controlling the curvature of corners.

Class Function
cnr:arch-none Square corner @ 0% radius
cnr:arch-small Round corner @ 0.3333rem radius
cnr:arch-medium Round corner @ 0.6666rem radius
cnr:arch-large Round corner @ 1rem radius
cnr:arch-full Round corner @ 100% radius
cnr:arch-top-small Round top corners @ 0.3333rem radius
cnr:arch-top-medium Round top corners @ 0.6666rem radius
cnr:arch-top-large Round top corners @ 1rem radius
cnr:arch-right-small Round right corners @ 0.3333rem radius
cnr:arch-right-medium Round right corners @ 0.6666rem radius
cnr:arch-right-large Round right corners @ 1rem radius
cnr:arch-bottom-small Round bottom corners @ 0.3333rem radius
cnr:arch-bottom-medium Round bottom corners @ 0.6666rem radius
cnr:arch-bottom-large Round bottom corners @ 1rem radius
cnr:arch-left-small Round left corners @ 0.3333rem radius
cnr:arch-left-medium Round left corners @ 0.6666rem radius
cnr:arch-left-large Round left corners @ 1rem radius

property: cnr
