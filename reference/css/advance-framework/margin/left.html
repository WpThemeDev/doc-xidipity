Description:Classes for controlling the left margin. An element's margin is the space between its border and other content.

Class Function
mar:left+0 No left margin
mar:left+0.25 Left margin @ +0.25rem
mar:left+0.5 Left margin @ +0.5rem
mar:left+0.75 Left margin @ +0.75rem
mar:left+1 Left margin @ +1rem
mar:left+1.25 Left margin @ +1.25rem
mar:left+1.5 Left margin @ +1.5rem
mar:left+1.75 Left margin @ +1.75rem
mar:left+2 Left margin @ +2rem
mar:left-0 No left margin

mar:left-0.5 Left margin @ -0.5rem
mar:left-0.75 Left margin @ -0.75rem
mar:left-1 Left margin @ -1rem
mar:left-1.25 Left margin @ -1.25rem
mar:left-1.5 Left margin @ -1.5rem

mar:left-2 Left margin @ -2rem
