Description:Classes for controlling the horizontal padding (left & right). An element's padding is the space between its content and its border.

Class Function
pad:hrz+0 No padding left & right
pad:hrz+0.25 Pad left & right @ +0.25rem
pad:hrz+0.5 Pad left & right @ +0.5rem
pad:hrz+0.75 Pad left & right @ +0.75rem
pad:hrz+1 Pad left & right @ +1rem
pad:hrz-1.25 Pad left & right @ +1.25rem
pad:hrz+1.5 Pad left & right @ +1.5rem
pad:hrz+1.75 Pad left & right @ +1.75rem
pad:hrz+2 Pad left & right @ +2rem

 

property: pad

property value:

hrz +0.25 to hrz +2 (rem)
