Description:Classes for controlling padding on the top. An element's padding is the space between its content and its border.

Class Function
pad:top+0 No top padding
pad:top+0.25 Pad top @ +0.25rem
pad:top+0.5 Pad top @ +0.5rem
pad:top+0.75 Pad top @ +0.75rem
pad:top+1 Pad top @ +1rem
pad:top+1.25 Pad top @ +1.25rem
pad:top+1.5 Pad top @ +1.5rem
pad:top+1.75 Pad top @ +1.75rem
pad:top+2 Pad top @ +2rem

 

property: pad

property value:

top +0.25 to top +2 (rem)
