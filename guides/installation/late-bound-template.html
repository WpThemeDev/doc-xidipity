Description:Late bound template installation directions.

 

A late bound template may be configured in the same manner as an early bound template, but may also be configured after adding it to your content. To illustrate let's use the the annotation template as an example. The following is the unedited thumbs up template as it will appear in the editor

 

​ Your content here …

 

The phrase "Your content here …" is editable and should be updated with the desired content.

 

Steps

  1. Identify desired template
  2. Highlight template
  3. Copy to clipboard
  4. Click the location where template will be applied
  5. Click the add template ( post_add ) toolbar icon
  6. Paste template into the tool
  7. Click OK button
  8. Perform final configuration
